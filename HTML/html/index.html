how it works

Monitor data usage by app

Discover how your data allowance is being used with Bill Shock's app by breakdown of your data usage. Find out which apps are using more data than expected and reduce wasted data use.

Understand your data usage history

Analyse your data usage with three months of historical usage data stored in the online dashboard. Use this data to ensure you're on the right mobile tariff for your needs, or find a better deal.

Data Roaming

If you regularly travel abroad you may faced increased data charges, avoid bill shocks with the data roaming monitoring and alerts.

Business users

Small business users can monitor all company mobile phone data usage through one easy to manage dashboard. Quick setup through CSV upload is available for multiple devices.
